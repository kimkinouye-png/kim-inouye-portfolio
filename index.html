Building Tools

Using Cursor, I created a custom capacity planner that helps teams understand the demand from a product roadmap, scope, and analyzes it against current capacity. Every product team is different, and the levers can make one designer slow will vary. So I included weighting that can be adjusted so give teams a more realistic view of their unique situation. This gives a clear understanding of the likely risks in different scenarios giving product leaders a thoughtful way to make informed decisions fast.

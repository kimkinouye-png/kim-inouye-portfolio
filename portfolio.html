Impact

Restore and Grow XO

Objective

Integrate Smart Wallet, Rewards, and Identity into checkout moments to reverse checkout erosion and strengthen the XO platform's flexibility.

What We Did

Designed a holistic ecosystem view that coordinates design efforts across multiple functions. The solution integrates BNPL (Pay in 4) directly into merchant checkout experiences with clear payment options and transparent terms.

Impact

  • Unified checkout experience across surfaces
  • Reduced checkout abandonment through integrated payment options
  • Strengthened design parity across the XO platform

Drive an Agentic Future - Pay Later

Objective

  • Seamless cross-platform payment orchestration

The Problem
300+ designers.
Zero visibility.
  • Work arrived via Slack from anywhere, anytime
  • No intake criteria — any request became a mandate
  • Managers couldn't see what teams were working on
  • Designers had no way to say no without being the bad guy
  • Constant context switching across projects
  • No shared language for sizing or handing off work

The Rule That Changed Everything
If it's not in the backlog,
it doesn't get design time.
Before
  • Any Slack message became a design request
  • No criteria for what was ready to design
  • Designers absorbed all political friction
After
  • Problem must be defined to enter the backlog
  • Designers could say "talk to Kim" and keep working
  • PMs had full visibility — no more black box

Case Study 01 · Discover
PRD to Design Brief
Problem
PRDs are technical documents not written customer-back. Designers couldn't tell if the problem was defined or if they were starting from scratch.
Solution
  • Agent turns PRD into clear design brief
  • Calls out gaps and missing customer context
  • Suggests questions and testing ideas
Status: Live
PRD Design Synthesizer
Generates actionable clarity from Product Requirement Documents — what matters, what's missing, what to ask.
Next: Slack integration · auto-reminders to partners
Case Study 02 · Deliver
Streamlining Legal
Root Cause
Wrong templates triggered wrong lawyers. Content designers copied old forms and missed fields. The process stalled before review even started.
Solution
  • MCP agent identifies correct template per review type
  • Fills forms completely — no tired eyes, no missed checkboxes
  • Error rate: zero

Appendix · Pilot in Progress
Vibe Prototyping
Problem
Prototyping is high effort, comes late in the design process, and is rarely experienced on actual devices in reviews.
Approach
  • Used Replit to generate working prototypes from design intent
  • Reduced design review cycles from 3 rounds to 1
  • Teams could iterate in the meeting itself
Status: In Progress
Key challenge
Design system changes faster than the prototype environment can track. Vibe prototyping works best when the design system is stable — or when the component set is scoped to the pilot.
Tools: Replit · Figma Make
Appendix · Pilot in Progress
Agentic Ideation
Problem
Design teams need to move faster to explore and refine ideas without losing momentum or customer focus. Blank canvas workshops are slow and synthesis is hard.
Approach
  • AI agents trained on PayPal customer personas
  • Designer inputs a problem statement
  • Agent generates ideas organized by persona perspective
  • Teams get a customer-grounded starting point in minutes
Status: In Progress
Why it matters
This isn't about saving time — it's about injecting quality and customer focus earlier in the process. A designer with a persona-grounded idea set makes better decisions faster.
Tools: FigJam AI · PayPal persona library
